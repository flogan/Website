Ideas

Professional Breeder Book Keeping

My mom breeds standard poodles for a living and is an entrepreneur turning it into a full business. However, none of her files are digital, and there is a lot of paperwork that comes with the field. There are contracts signed with the sale of a puppy, vet records, microchip numbers, pedigrees, etc. Whenever my mother needs a piece of information that isn't readily available she has to flip through massive binders to locate a document. The idea here would be to digitize records and scan documents to be able to search by dog or customer to quickly obtain records, created connections between dogs like parents and breeding offspring to quickly access important information.

Is This Recyclable? App

The idea would be to create an app for UMass students which utilizes AI and image recognition to tell users if an image they take of a piece of waste is either recyclable, compostable or must be put in the trash at UMass. This idea came from trying to increase recycling participation after seeing the recycling rate numbers.

If you have any tips or ideas on where to start or some technologies to use with these, or want to help contribute, feel free to contact me
